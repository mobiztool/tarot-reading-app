# Content QA Checklist - Encyclopedia Cards

## Card Review Checklist

Use this checklist for reviewing each of the 78 tarot cards.

### Basic Information
- [ ] Thai name is correct and commonly used
- [ ] English name matches traditional naming
- [ ] Card number is correct (Major Arcana)
- [ ] Suit/Element is correct (Minor Arcana)
- [ ] Image displays correctly

### Keywords
- [ ] Upright keywords: 3-5 relevant keywords
- [ ] Reversed keywords: 3-5 relevant keywords
- [ ] Keywords are unique (not duplicated from other cards)
- [ ] Keywords match traditional interpretations

### Meanings
- [ ] Upright meaning: Clear and complete (2-3 paragraphs)
- [ ] Reversed meaning: Clear and complete (2-3 paragraphs)
- [ ] Meanings align with traditional Tarot interpretations
- [ ] Language is accessible and easy to understand
- [ ] Tone is encouraging and balanced

### Application Sections
- [ ] Love & Relationships: Specific and helpful
- [ ] Career & Finance: Practical advice
- [ ] Health: Appropriate (not medical advice)
- [ ] General advice: Actionable

### Quality Standards
- [ ] No grammatical errors
- [ ] No spelling mistakes
- [ ] No plagiarism (verified)
- [ ] Consistent with other cards
- [ ] SEO keywords included naturally

### Imagery & Symbolism
- [ ] Key symbols explained
- [ ] Colors and elements described
- [ ] Connection to card meaning clear
