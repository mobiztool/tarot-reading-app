# Story 3.11: Share Incentives & Gamification

## Status

**Approved**

## Story

**As a** user, **I want** to feel rewarded when I share, **so that** I'm motivated to spread the word.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Share prompt: "Love your reading? Share it!"
2. Share badge: unlock after first share
3. Social proof: "X shared today"
4. Thank you message after share
5. Referral tracking foundation
6. Sharing stats in profile
7. Share streaks (future)
8. Leaderboard (future consideration)
9. Never auto-post (user-initiated only)
10. Respectful UX: dismissible prompts
