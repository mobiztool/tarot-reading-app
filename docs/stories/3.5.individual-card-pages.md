# Story 3.5: Tarot Encyclopedia - Individual Card Pages

## Status

**Approved**

## Story

**As a** user, **I want** to read detailed information about each tarot card, **so that** I can understand its symbolism and apply it to my life.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Card detail page (`/cards/[slug]`) for each of 78 cards
2. SEO-friendly slugs: `/cards/the-fool`, `/cards/ace-of-cups`
3. Content: large image, name, number/suit, upright meaning, reversed meaning, keywords, symbolism, advice
4. Related cards section: "Similar energy cards"
5. CTA: "Get your reading with this card"
6. Breadcrumbs: Home > Encyclopedia > [Card Name]
7. Previous/Next card navigation
8. Mobile responsive: well-formatted on mobile
9. SEO: "[Card Name] Tarot Meaning - [App]", H1/H2 hierarchy, structured data
10. Social sharing buttons on card pages
11. Internal linking to related content
12. Print-friendly CSS
