# Story 3.2: Social Media Share Buttons & Integration

## Status

**Approved**

## Story

**As a** user, **I want** easy-to-use share buttons after my reading, **so that** I can quickly post to my preferred social platform.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Share buttons: Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, Copy Link
2. Instagram: downloads image (no web sharing API)
3. Facebook: share dialog with image and text
4. Twitter: compose with image and text
5. Copy Link: copies URL with success toast
6. Default share text: "[App Name] revealed [Card Name] âœ¨"
7. Text customizable on supported platforms
8. Image attached automatically
9. Analytics: platform, completed, cancelled
10. Web Share API: use native share on mobile
11. Fallback: manual screenshot instructions
12. Button styling: prominent, clear icons
