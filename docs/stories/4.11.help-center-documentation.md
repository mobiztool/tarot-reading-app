# Story 4.11: Help Center & Documentation

## Status

**Approved**

## Story

**As a** user who needs help, **I want** easy access to support, **so that** I solve problems quickly.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Help Center page (`/help`)
2. FAQ categories: Getting Started, Account, Readings, Privacy, Technical
3. 15-20 FAQ entries
4. Search FAQs
5. Contact support form/email
6. Video tutorials (optional)
7. Troubleshooting guide
8. Privacy policy: comprehensive, PDPA-compliant
9. Terms of service
10. Help button: accessible from all pages
11. Contextual help: tooltips
12. Analytics: most viewed articles
13. Feedback: "Was this helpful?"
