# Story 3.10: Analytics & Growth Metrics Dashboard

## Status

**Approved**

## Story

**As a** product manager, **I want** visibility into content performance, **so that** we optimize growth strategy.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. GA4 events: share_initiated, share_completed, platform
2. Encyclopedia analytics: views per card, most viewed
3. Blog analytics: views, read time, scroll depth
4. Referral tracking: UTM parameters
5. Conversion funnel: content → reading → signup
6. SEO metrics: organic traffic, keywords
7. Engagement: bounce rate, pages/session
8. Content performance report
9. Share virality metric
10. Dashboard: PostHog or Vercel Analytics
11. Weekly reports (future)
12. A/B testing infrastructure
