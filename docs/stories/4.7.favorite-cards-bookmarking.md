# Story 4.7: Favorite Cards & Bookmarking

## Status

**Approved**

## Story

**As a** user, **I want** to bookmark favorite readings and cards, **so that** I can return to meaningful insights.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Favorite button on reading results: heart icon
2. Favorite button on card encyclopedia
3. Favorites in profile: "My Favorites"
4. List: bookmarked readings and cards
5. Unfavorite option
6. Count: "X favorites"
7. Quick access from profile menu
8. Analytics: most favorited cards
9. No limit for logged-in users
10. Share favorites feature
