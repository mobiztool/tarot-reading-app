# Story 4.4: Advanced Card Animations & Micro-interactions

## Status

**Approved**

## Story

**As a** user, **I want** smooth, delightful animations, **so that** the experience feels magical and premium.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Enhanced card flip: 3D perspective, shadow
2. Card hover: float and shimmer
3. Page transitions: smooth fade/slide
4. Loading: mystical spinner or particles
5. Button interactions: scale, glow
6. Success: subtle celebration confetti
7. Toasts: slide in with bounce
8. Scroll: parallax effects
9. Haptic feedback on mobile
10. Performance: 60fps on mid-range devices
11. Reduced motion: respect accessibility
12. Framer Motion consistency
13. Sound effects (optional, mutable)
