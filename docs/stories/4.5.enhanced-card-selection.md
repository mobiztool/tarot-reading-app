# Story 4.5: Enhanced Card Selection Experience

## Status

**Approved**

## Story

**As a** user, **I want** interactive card selection, **so that** choosing feels meaningful and intuitive.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Fan layout: arc formation
2. Drag-to-select: drag card to selection area
3. Swipe gestures: browse cards (mobile)
4. Hover effects: lift and glow
5. Selection feedback: golden glow
6. Shuffle animation: visual shuffle
7. Sound: shuffling sound (toggleable)
8. Guided: tooltip "Choose card that calls to you"
9. Timer: gentle timer (optional)
10. Undo selection
11. Multiple modes: tap OR drag
12. Performance: smooth on mobile
